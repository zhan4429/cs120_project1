Members

Ashley has been classically trained upon the violin in the Suzuki Method at Indiana University beginning at the age of four years old and has performed with various orchestras throughout the Midwest. Her college years were spent in Northern Indiana attending Saint Mary’s College, Notre Dame where she was a member of the Notre Dame rowing team. After graduating Law School and relocating to Carmel, Indiana to practice law, she became active on the Board of Directors for the Center for the Performing Arts at the Palladium in Carmel, Indiana. In 2020, while spending time with friends, Ashley decided to apply her talents to the electric violin in order to play rock and country music, and Six Figures was born!

Bart has been a music instructor in the Indianapolis area for 25 years. He teaches guitar, bass guitar, piano, and voice lessons. He also performs in bands around town. He has performed all over the country touring with American Idol Finalist Amanda Overmyer. He has also released his own music for licensing on tv and radio. You can catch a song of his on Showtime’s The Real L Word, and has also had music performed on MTV and VH1 to name a few.

Chris is from Carmel, IN but currently resides in Indianapolis. He has spent years living in places all over the country like Seattle, Port Townsend, Minneapolis, St. Paul, Kinnic Falls, and Bloomington, Indiana. He also works in real estate as well as volunteers for addiction issues all over the state. He first learned the piano at around 5 years old and didn’t even pick up a guitar until around high school. Though the ground work to be able to utilize the music theory was learned at a young age, it wasn’t until roughly 2016 that he put those skills to good use. Since then he Chris has played at local fundraisers, gigs and festivals around the Indy area including opening for Jenny Devoe.

Kevin is a singer songwriter from Indianapolis. His love for music came early and developed in school as a drummer in the marching band in junior high and high school. 6 state championships and a trip to New York to March in the Macys thanksgiving day parade are part of his experience. Though he never quit playing the drums, singing became his strength in music and led to writing and recording later in life. He has shared a stage with Sammy Hagar who played guitar for Kevin’s original song called Diamond Eyes. He recorded songs with Henry Lee Summer and is currently working on many more songs. Kevin sings lead vocals for Six Figures and is currently working on some new originals with the band.

Mike was a drum virtuoso at a young age and…just kidding. Mike is just a guy who loves to play drums. He started playing the piano at age 4 and drums at age 10. Early influences were Neil Peart, Dave Weckl, Alex Van Halen and Tommy Lee. He’s played 80s and 90s rock and alternative rock in previous bands. Mike’s playlist includes Breaking Benjamin, Alice In Chains, Tool, GnR, Night Ranger, Motley Crue, Huey Lewis, Our Lady Peace, Foo Fighters (RIP), OAR, Dave, and Rush. Mike has played the local and regional scene of Indiana with American Cheese, Band Wagon, and Ransom Note. He also plays CCM at his local church.

Martin started playing guitar in high school but took a long 20 year pause in music. Then he moved back to Indiana and was convinced by his brother Bart to pick up a bass and join Six Figures. This was 2 years ago and now Martin is the full time bass player in Six Figures Band.
